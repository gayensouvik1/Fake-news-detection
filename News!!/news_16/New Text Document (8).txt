Nokia 9 rumoured to come with QHD OLED display, Iris scanner, Android Nougat 7.1.2

If the recent releases from Nokia have kept you interested in its smartphones, then we have some more news to keep you hooked onto it.

Apparently, HMD Global-backed Nokia is secretly working on its premium 2017 flagship smartphone model, according to a report by Nokia Power User (NPU).

Dubbed Nokia 9, the smartphone is power packed with premium features such an iris Scanner, dual-cameras with Carl Zeiss optics, a QHD OLED display, water-and-dust resistant capabilities, and Nokia “OZO” audio technology.

On the specifications front, the high-end smartphone will have a QHD 5.5-inch OLED display,Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 processor, Adreno 540 GPU, a massive 6GB of RAM, either 64GB or 128GB of internal storage,a 3,800mAh battery with Quick Charge 4.0 support, IP68 water-and-dust resistant certification, a fingerprint scanner, and iris scanner, suggests the NPU leaks. The smartphone is expected to run Android 7.1.2 Nougat out of the box.

Nokia 9 may be the first device to feature “Nokia OZO audio” enhancements system claim NPU. It is basically, a software-based technology that promises to enable any camera or smartphone to record 3D audio or spatial sound. The 3D audio technology will be compatible with smartphones with two or three microphones.

On the camera front, Nokia 9 is expected to come with a 22MP dual lens Carl Zeiss rear camera along with a 12MP front facing camera for selfies.

While the Nokia 9 surely looks to be a very exciting smartphone, there are no confirmed reports about the launch. Therefore, we suggest that you take this information with a pinch of salt, as all the information is based on leaks and rumors.
