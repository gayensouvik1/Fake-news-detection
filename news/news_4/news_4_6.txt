In a surprising development, the US President Donald Trump called up Prime Minister Narendra Modi and congratulated him for BJP’s victory in recently concluded Assembly polls.

PTI sources have confirmed the development and quoted White House for the news update. Narendra Modi led BJP is in centre since 2014 and scored a tremendous victory in recently concluded assembly election. The BJP won four out of five states in the assembly elections.
This call can be seen as a strategic indication of increasing friendship between both leaders. Former US President Barack Obama had very cordial relationship with PM Moodi and Trump seems to follow just that.
