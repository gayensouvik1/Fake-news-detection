US President Donald Trump today called Prime Minister Narendra Modi and congratulated him on his success in recent assembly polls, the White House said.

Following the elections held in five states, Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) formed the government in four states: Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Goa and Manipur.

It, however, lost Punjab to Congress.

The BJP returned to power in the politically-crucial state of UP after a gap of 15 years.

In Goa and Manipur, BJP managed to form the government with the support of other parties, after the verdict threw hung result.
