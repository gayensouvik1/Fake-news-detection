At least 400 jawans attached to the CRPF camp at Pallipuram were taken ill due to suspected food poisoning, the police said. They were admitted to various hospitals in the city following complaints of diarrhoea and vomiting after consuming food on Saturday. However, initial investigations suggested that the jawans fell ill after consuming fish curry.

An enquiry has been ordered in the CRPF jawans food poisoning case and food sample have been taken for the investigation.

109 jawans are under observation at Trivandrum Medical College Hospital. State Health Minister K K Shylaja visited them at the hospital Saturday night. While 200 jawans have been admitted to the Thiruvananthapuram Medical College Hospital. Rest are being treated at various hospitals.
